## Led

The LEDs must be installed on the bottom side of the PCB with the light guide facing upward. Align the angled (cut) pin on the LED with the round pad on the board, and repeat this step for all LEDs. After that, simply solder them in place.

![led_instal](../doc/img/build/stone/led_install.png)
![led_soldered](../doc/img/build/stone/led_soldered.jpg)

## MCU Board
>
> [!WARNING]
> BE CAREFUL AND USE EYE PROTECTION WHEN CUTTING PLASTIC AND PINS. When trimming, cover the area with your hand to reduce the risk of small pieces of plastic or metal pins flying off toward your face or monitor.

Solder the jumpers on the underside of the PCB near the controller pins.

![jumper_patch.](../doc/img/build/stone/jumper_patch.png)
![jumper_soldered.jpg](../doc/img/build/stone/jumper_soldered.jpg)

Then install the headers with the socket facing upward and solder the pin supports (also from the underside). Leave the space for the battery pins empty and do not install any pins there.

![header_install](../doc/img/build/stone/header_install.jpg)
![bat_pin](../doc/img/build/stone/bat_pin.jpg)

Next, take a strip of pins and remove them from the plastic. Install the controller and insert the separated pins into the headers. Using side cutters, trim the pins flush with the controller, then solder them in place. [see details here](../doc/socket.md)

![pin_striped](../doc/img/build/stone/pin_striped.jpg)
![pin_install](../doc/img/build/stone/pin_install.jpg)
![pin_soldered](../doc/img/build/stone/pin_soldered.jpg)

## Button and slider

Install the reset button and the power switch, then simply solder them in place. For convenience, solder the switch first and then the button.

![button_soldered](../doc/img/build/stone/button_soldered.jpg)

## Switch

Install the switches on the top side and solder them from the underside of the PCB.

![switch_install](../doc/img/build/stone/switch_install.jpg)

## Case

Insert the magnets into the case, then place the nuts from the opposite side. Install the PCB and tighten the screws.

![magnet_install](../doc/img/build/stone/magnet_install.jpg)
![nut_install](../doc/img/build/stone/nut_install.jpg)
![screw_install](../doc/img/build/stone/screw_install.jpg)

## Finish

Install the keycaps. Then connect the controller to your computer with a cable, press the reset button twice, and flash the firmware.

**Congratulations, you’ve assembled your РИСАТЕНО!**
